Highlights - England v Australia Day 1

Star Sports 2
  • share Share
4,983,187 views 17 Jun 2023, Live! Podcast on Star Sports 2

The Ashes is a historic and highly competitive cricket series played between England and Australia. The series is one of the most prestigious events in cricket, and it takes place alternately in England and Australia.

The rivalry between England and Australia in The Ashes dates back to 1882 when Australia won a Test match in England for the first time. A satirical obituary published in a British newspaper declared that English cricket had died and "the body will be cremated and the ashes taken to Australia." Thus, the term "The Ashes" was coined, and the contest between the two teams has carried that name ever since. The Ashes series consists of five Test matches, with the team that wins the majority of the matches taking home the coveted Ashes urn.

The series is known for its intense battles, exceptional performances, and memorable moments that have become part of cricketing folklore. Matches between England and Australia in The Ashes are eagerly anticipated by cricket fans worldwide. The rivalry sparks fierce competition, and players from both teams strive to showcase their skills and outperform their opponents. The series has produced legendary cricketers, iconic innings, and unforgettable moments that have left a lasting impact on the sport.

Whether it's the fierce bowling spells, dramatic batting displays, or captivating fielding moments, The Ashes continues to captivate cricket fans with its rich history and thrilling contests.
